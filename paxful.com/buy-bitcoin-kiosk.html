Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How much do I get paid, how often and how?

You get 2% from the total volume of every trade each and every time someone you refer buys bitcoin for up to a year from their first trade.

Q. How often do I get paid?

You get a cut each time a trade is made in real time. It goes straight to your wallet - ready to be spent!

Q. How is the money paid out?

Your earnings are stored as bitcoin in your Paxful wallet. It can be used or spent immediately. You can also sell on Paxful for a fee to bitcoin buyers, making even more!

Q. I am a Paxful vendor, can I use this as a checkout on my site that goes right to my offers?

Yes! Paxful checkout can easily be made to show only your offers if you pass your offer ID as an argument and only your specific offer will be shown. It will basically serve as an escrow plugin for your site and your customers won't ever think they left your website. Refer to our developer docs how to customize the widget.

Q. How much can I hope to earn? And how do I know?

How much you can earn depends on the kind of traffic your site gets and how prominently you can Paxful widget.

Q. I have a bitcoin startup and my users need bitcoin. Can I use this as a tool for our users to buy bitcoin?

Totally! Paxful widget and its Bitcoin Kiosk was initially designed for other bitcoin startups to monetize their great content and apps. From a simple blog to advanced app or bitcoin wallet, Paxful buy bitcoin widget is perfect way for your users to buy their bitcoins right within your app. Plus you will make 2% on every bitcoin purchase made by your users!

Q. How do I track my earnings?

Every time a trade completes successfully you will get an email and your balance rise within the Paxful dashboard.

Q. How much control do I have? Do my visitors ever leave my site?

Visitors never leave your website. If you choose to use a link, the Paxful window opens up as an iframe in all browsers except Chrome. For Chrome users, it will open up as a new window.
